BYZANTINE Quiz Bowl Tournament

Welcome to BYZANTINE!

Bringing Young Zealous Academics Needing Tough Intellectual Nuances and Excellence

Hosted online by Winston Churchill Middle School on March 22, 2025, BYZANTINE is a quiz bowl tournament designed for middle school teams and high school novice teams.

The tournament will use the TSE MS #1 question set, and statistics will be uploaded to hsquizbowl.org after the event.

Eligibility: Open to middle school teams and high school novice teams. Unsure if your team qualifies? Contact us on Discord at quizbowlenjoyer on discord or byzantineqb@gmail.com on gmail.

Important: All players must have at least one hand visible on camera at all times during matches.
